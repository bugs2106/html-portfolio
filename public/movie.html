The Best Movies According to Bhakti

My top 3 movies of all-time.

1. Wednesday

A dark, quirky series with a perfect mix of mystery and humor. Loved the aesthetic and Wednesday’s bold personality.

2. Fantastic Four

A fun superhero movie with cool powers and a light, adventurous vibe. Easy watch with entertaining action.

3. Mission Impossible - Dead Reckoning

Fast-paced action with intense stunts and slick spy moments. Classic Tom Cruise chaos — very entertaining to watch.
